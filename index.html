About Us

Sri Padmavathi Exports is a trusted agricultural export company based in Padur, Tamil Nadu. We supply premium quality Indian agricultural products - from our fields in Padur to customers across Asia, Europe, Africa, and the Middle East. We believe in farm-fresh quality, timely service, and building global trust.
